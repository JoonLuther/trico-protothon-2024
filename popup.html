Feminist Theory

Feminist theory is a dynamic framework that analyzes and challenges social structures perpetuating gender inequality, advocating for the empowerment of women and marginalized genders. It encompasses diverse perspectives aimed at reshaping societal norms, fostering inclusivity, and striving towards a more equitable world for all individuals, regardless of gender.


Algorithmic Bias

Algorithmic bias refers to the inherent prejudices embedded in automated systems, perpetuating inequality and discrimination in various aspects of life, from hiring decisions to criminal justice outcomes. Understanding and mitigating algorithmic bias is crucial for creating fairer and more just societies, where technology is harnessed to promote equality and opportunity for all.
